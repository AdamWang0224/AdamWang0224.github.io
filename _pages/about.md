I am a Graduate Research Fellow and Visiting Researcher at INSAIT, where I work with Prof. [Luc Van Gool](https://insait.ai/prof-luc-van-gool/) and Dr. [Danda Paudel](https://insait.ai/dr-danda-paudel/) on 3D vision. My work sits at the intersection of computer vision and machine learning, with a particular interest in geometry-aware learning, generative modeling, and healthcare applications.

I recently completed my MPhil in Data Intensive Science at the University of Cambridge. During this period, I also worked as a research intern at MMLAB@CUHK with Prof. [Xiangyu Yue](https://xyue.io/) and Dr. [Wei-Hong Li](https://weihonglee.github.io/). Before that, I received my BSc in Mathematics in 2024 from Harbin Institute of Technology.

In my final undergraduate year, I received the Singapore International Pre-Graduate Award ([SIPGA](https://www.a-star.edu.sg/Scholarships/for-graduate-studies/singapore-international-graduate-award-singa)). I worked as a research assistant at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), A*STAR, supervised by Principal Scientist [Jun Cheng](https://samjcheng.github.io/), and as a research intern at the School of Medicine, National University of Singapore, supervised by Prof. [Dianbo Liu](http://www.cogai4sci.com/).

I enjoy building vision systems that are both mathematically grounded and practically useful. At the moment, I am especially interested in 3D perception, representation learning, and generative approaches that can improve visual understanding under real-world constraints.
