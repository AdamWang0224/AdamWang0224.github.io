I am a Graduate Research Fellow and Visiting Researcher at INSAIT, where I work with Prof. [Luc Van Gool](https://insait.ai/prof-luc-van-gool/) and Dr. [Danda Paudel](https://insait.ai/dr-danda-paudel/) on 3D vision.

I recently completed my MPhil in Data Intensive Science at the University of Cambridge. During that period, I also interned at MMLAB@CUHK with Prof. [Xiangyu Yue](https://xyue.io/) and Dr. [Wei-Hong Li](https://weihonglee.github.io/).

Before that, I received my BSc in Mathematics from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2024. In my final undergraduate year, I received the Singapore International Pre-Graduate Award ([SIPGA](https://www.a-star.edu.sg/Scholarships/for-graduate-studies/singapore-international-graduate-award-singa)) and worked at I2R, A*STAR with Dr. [Jun Cheng](https://samjcheng.github.io/) and at NUS with Prof. [Dianbo Liu](http://www.cogai4sci.com/).

My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning, especially 3D vision, generative models, and healthcare-related applications.
