This paper investigates the mean-square exponential stability of multi-linked stochastic delayed
complex networks with stochastic hybrid impulses. The analysis combines Dupire Ito's formula,
Lyapunov methods, graph theory, and stochastic analysis to derive sufficient stability criteria.
